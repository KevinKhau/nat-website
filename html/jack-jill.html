Client

|   Jack N' Jill Philippines

Task

|   Increase product line awareness during events and holidays

Jack N’ Jill chocolates are known for their afforable brands of chocolates. Premium, imported brands are starting to be aggressive in terms of their communication and availability- The task was to increase awareness of their chocolate brands, especially during events where chocolates are relevant.

Two spots have been created- all focusing on staying ‘sweet’ even on rough days. The first digital spots revolves around valentines, communicating that you don’t have to stay bitter even if you don’t have a date this valentines

Another spot, which is yet to be released- features how easy negativity can spread throughout the office, but even easier to liven up with chocolates for everyone.
