Client

|   Bayer Philippines

Task

|   Launch Canesten Philippines in various digital platforms

Canesten®, the Philippines’ and Europe’s number 1 topical cream wanted their presense felt on digital. The launch campaign consisted of the creation of a social media playbook for their new FB and YT channels, as well as a highly successful TikTok hashtag campaign that went on to be the benchmark for future TikTok campaigns in the Philippines.
